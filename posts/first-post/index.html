First post: baked brie

09 Dec 2018

Hey, it’s baked brie. Here’s how to make it:

  • Slice the top off of a wheel of brie
  • Sandwich marionberries in between the brie
  • Wrap the whole thing in fathead dough
  • Brush with egg whites, and sprinkle sesame seeds on top
  • Cut a couple of vents in the top

We ate the whole thing over the course of the day. No need for dipping foods, just slice off a hunk.
